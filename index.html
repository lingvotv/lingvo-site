1. Download chrome extension
2. Open "chrome://extensions" or go to extensions over menu.
3. Drag & Drop the extension.crx file into the chrome settings page we opened before.
3. Go to the app in any browser (tested in mobile safary/chrome)
5. Open netflix, watch movie, pick english subtitles
6. See subtitles on the phone, click on any word to translate, click on multiple words to translate them together.
