How this works

Watch a movie with sound and subtitles both in the language you aim to learn.

It makes understanding easier and activates your visual memory!
LingvoTV App on your phone receives subtitles from the movie while you watch it.

Click on any word or words combination to translate them without stopping the movie!
The most important thing is to have fun!

Don't worry if you can't translate every word!
Language App Translate

Installation

Install LingvoTV Chrome Extension.

It is required to send the subtitles to your phone.

Connect LingvoTV app on your phone and your Chrome Extension.

Click on Extension to show the code. Enter this code in the app.

Open a movie in Chrome using one of the supported services.

Select sound and subtitles both in the language you want to learn.

Start playing movie. Once you see subtitles on the screen in the movie, you should see the same subtitles on your phone.

Now you can click on words to translate them.
