How Extension Connect Language

Lingvo.tv app receives subtitles from a Netflix movie while you watch it.

It allows you to click on any word or words combination to translate it.

INSTALL CHROME EXTENSION

Extension allows you to receive the subtitles in your app.

Currently only Chrome browser is supported.

Connect Lingvo.tv App and Extension together.

Click on Extension, it will display verification code.

Open www.lingvo.tv on your phone. Click on "OPEN APP" and enter verification code.

Now you will receive subtitles once you play the movie.

Select your source and destination language.

Subtitles is the source.
