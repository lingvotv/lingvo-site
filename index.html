How Extension Connect Language

Sound and subtitles in the movie should be both in the language you want to learn.

Lingvo TV app on your phone receives subtitles from the movie while you watch it

and allows you to click on any word or words combination to translate them, without stopping the movie!

INSTALL CHROME EXTENSION

Extension allows you to receive the subtitles on your phone.

Watch movie in the same browser where you install the extension.

Currently only Chrome browser is supported.

Connect Lingvo TV App and the Browser.
  • Open www.lingvo.tv on your phone and OPEN APP.
  • Open Desktop Chrome Browser and click on Lingvo TV Extension.
  • Enter the code displayed in Extension into the app.
Done, now you will receive subtitles once you play the movie.

Select your source and destination language.
